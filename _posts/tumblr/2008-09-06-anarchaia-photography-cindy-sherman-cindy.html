Photography: Cindy Sherman

Cindy Sherman
Untitled Film Still #50
1979

Cindy Sherman began her now famous series Untitled Film Stills twenty years ago at the end of 1977. Those small black-and-white photographs of Sherman spoke to a generation of baby boomer women who had grown up absorbing those glamorous images at home on their televisions, taking such portrayals as cues for their future.

Bartolomeu de Gusmão

Bartolomeu de Gusmão, born Bartolomeu Lourenço de Gusmão (1685, Santos, São Paulo, Brazil – November 18, 1724, Toledo, Spain), was a priest and naturalist born in the Portuguese colony of Brazil, recalled for his early work on lighter-than-air airship design.

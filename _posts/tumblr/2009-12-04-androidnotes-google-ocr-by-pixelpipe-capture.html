androidnotes:

Google OCR by Pixelpipe

Capture printed text from your Android device and upload directly to your Google Docs account for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) as editable text.

The GDocs OCR feature is currently in beta however good result can be obtained when uploading large clearly printed text. Screenshots of web pages and documents work great

Add Russian Translation to Google Talk!

  • Add XX2YY@bot.talk.google.com to your buddylist. XX will be your language such as EN(which stands for English) and YY is the language you wish to translate to such as RU(which is Russian)

  • Send the phrase you wish to translate to that person and almost instantly you will get a response back with your translation!

  • You can even start a group chat and add multiple translation bots to it to get your phrases translated in many languages at the same time!

Examples:

  • en2ru@bot.talk.google.com (English to Russian)
  • ru2en@bot.talk.google.com (Russian to English)

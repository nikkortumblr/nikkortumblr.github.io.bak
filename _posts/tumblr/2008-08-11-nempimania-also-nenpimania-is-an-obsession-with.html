Nempimania (also Nenpimania) is an obsession with getting the best fuel economy possible from a hybrid car. It is derived from the Japanese “nempi” (燃費)—a contraction of nenryōshōhiryō （燃料消費量）[4] meaning fuel economy, and mania, meaning “craze for.” Nempimania is exhibited by owners of the Toyota Prius and other hybrid owners by various habits aimed at maximizing fuel economy: slow starts, “Pulse and Glide”, timing stoplights, driving barefoot[citation needed], etc.

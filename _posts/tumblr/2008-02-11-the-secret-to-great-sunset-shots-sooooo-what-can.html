The Secret to Great Sunset Shots Sooooo… What can you do to make your sunset shots stand out? What technique can you make use of that will make your sunset images stand out from all the gazillions of other beach shots, palm tree shots, observation deck pictures, and mountain top photos that are out there? Simple… Don’t shoot the sunset. Instead, take your pictures long after the sun has set.
Looooooooong after the sun has set. (via Twilight Photography Tips)

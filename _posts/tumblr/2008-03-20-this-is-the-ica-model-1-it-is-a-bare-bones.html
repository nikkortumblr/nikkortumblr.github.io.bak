This is the Ica model #1! It is a bare bones dirt-cheap box-type entry level camera for the format of 4.5x6cm, vertically oriented, a format considered “miniature” in this time. Still, it was three times larger than our “modern” 35mm film format! (via Ica cameras of 1913)

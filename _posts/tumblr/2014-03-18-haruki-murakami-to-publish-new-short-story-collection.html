murakamistuff:

Haruki Murakami will have his first short story collection in nine years published in roughly a month from now: Onna no Inai Otoko-tachi (“Men Who Do Not Have Girlfriends”). The collection will contain five stories previously released in various magazines and one unreleased piece.

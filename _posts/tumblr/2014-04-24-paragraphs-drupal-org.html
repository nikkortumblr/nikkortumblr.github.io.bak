Paragraphs is the new way of content creation! Instead of putting all your content in one WYSIWYG body field, your content will exist of a paragraph field containing multiple paragraph items. Paragraph items can contain multiple fields and have different types of field sets(bundles), which makes your content extremely flexible!

Some examples:

Add a slideshow between blocks of text
Add a youtube embed between your text
Add quotes between your content blocks
Check the images on the right to see the resulting node form.

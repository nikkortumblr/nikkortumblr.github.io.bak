Anri Sala (born 1974, Tirana, Albania) is a contemporary artist. He studied art at the Albanian Academy of Arts from 1992 - 1996. He has also studied video at the Ecole Nationale des Arts Decoratifs, Paris and film directory in Le Fresnoy-Studio National des Arts Contemporains, Tourcoing. He lives and works in Paris, France. His primary medium is that of video.
